About Gemini Chat

Gemini Chat is an interactive chatbot application powered by Google's Gemini AI model, built using LangChain and Django.

Our mission is to provide a seamless conversational experience, leveraging state-of-the-art language models for natural and helpful interactions.

This project demonstrates how to integrate advanced AI with modern web frameworks for real-world applications.

For more details on Gemini AI, visit Google AI.
